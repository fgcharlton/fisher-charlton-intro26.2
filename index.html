About

I am a passionate public health data professional. I aim to translate data into actionable insights that helps drive decision-making for communities. I also believe that communities should feel empowered to use data to amplify their stories, celebrate their successes, and highlight their challenges.

With this passion for accessible data, I enrolled in Code the Dream's Data Engineering Program to further grow my data analysis and visualization skills.
